Bennett Pyro Mondstadt
Benny's Adventure Team is an exception to the norm in the Adventurer's Guild, Mondstadt Branch.
Initially, the team mechanism requiring at least 3 or 4 regulars in each team was set up to promote
mutual support and prevent accidents. As for Benny's Adventure Team, however, its only regular
member has been Bennett himself. The lack of team members essentially has everything to do with
Bennett's infamous bad luck. However, being the passionate adventurer he is, Bennett never feels
lonely. Well, maybe once or twice, sometimes...
