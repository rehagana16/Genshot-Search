Qiqi Cryo Liyue
An apprentice and herb gatherer at Bubu Pharmacy. "Blessed" by the adepti with a body that
cannot die, this petite zombie cannot do anything without first giving herself orders to do it.
Qiqi's memory is like a sieve. Out of necessity, she always carries around a notebook in which
she writes anything important that she is sure to forget later. But on her worst days, she even
forgets to look at her notebook...
