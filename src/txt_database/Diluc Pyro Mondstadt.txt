Diluc Pyro Mondstadt
As the city of ballads and wines, Mondstadt's alcohol industry is renowned all over Teyvat. As the owner of the Dawn Winery, Diluc is essentially in charge of the industry. This means he has a huge
stream of revenue and an entire network of information in the form of tavern patrons' gossip right at
his fingertips. In a way, he is the uncrowned king of Mondstadt.
