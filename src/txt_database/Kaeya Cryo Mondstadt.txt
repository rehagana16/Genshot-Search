Kaeya Cryo Mondstadt
Kaeya Alberich is an adopted son to the Ragnvindr Family, the renowned wine tycoons. It has been
a long time since he last called Diluc Ragnvindr "brother". Kaeya currently serves as the Cavalry
Captain of the Knights of Favonius, trusted by Jean. Kaeya is often the one wrapping things up in
every incident that occurs in the vicinity of Mondstadt.
